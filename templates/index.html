About Jasmine
Jasmine Paolini is an Italian professional tennis player born in Castel nuovo di Garfagnana on January 4, 1996. She grew up in Bagni di Lucca, where she started playing tennis at a young age.
She is coached by Sara Errani and trains mainly in Forte dei Marmi. Her mother Jacqueline has Ghanaian and Polish origins, while her father Ugo is Italian. Paolini has often cited Roger Federer as her tennis idol and speaks Italian, English and Polish.
